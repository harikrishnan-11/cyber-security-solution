FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can Stackly deploy protection?

We can deploy sensor coverage and begin 24/7 monitoring within 24 hours of contract signing. Full onboarding — including SIEM integration and policy tuning — is completed within 5 business days.

What's your incident response time SLA?

Our Defend Pro plan guarantees a 4-hour response SLA, and Defend Elite guarantees 1-hour. For emergency incidents, our 24/7 hotline connects you to a senior analyst in under 3 minutes, guaranteed.

Do you work with small businesses?

Our Defend Basic plan is designed for organizations with 10–50 endpoints. We also offer project-based engagements (pen tests, audits) for smaller teams that don't need ongoing managed services.

Can Stackly help with compliance audits?

Yes. We have a dedicated GRC team with expertise in SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS, HIPAA, and GDPR. We've helped 100+ companies pass their first audit on the first attempt.

Do you offer a free security assessment?

Yes. We offer a free 48-hour attack surface assessment to all prospective clients. This includes an external scan of your exposed assets and a prioritized findings report with remediation guidance.

Is there a long-term contract commitment?

No long-term contracts required. All managed service plans are month-to-month. We believe our results speak for themselves — our 94% annual retention rate proves it.
