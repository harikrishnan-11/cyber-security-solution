Mission & Vision

We Exist to Make the
Internet Safer

Founded in 2008 by a team of ex-NSA analysts and offensive security researchers, Stackly has spent over a decade building the tools, processes, and intelligence networks that modern enterprises need to survive an increasingly hostile digital world.

Our approach is adversarial-first: we build our defenses by thinking the way attackers think, using the same tools, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) that real threat actors use in the wild.
